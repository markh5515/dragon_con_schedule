👥 Group Members

📝 How to Use the Group Planner:

  • Add Group Members: Enter names and pick colors for each person in your group above
  • Assign Attendees: Click on attendee badges under each event to toggle who's interested
  • See Conflicts: When multiple people want different events at the same time, you'll see a conflict summary
  • Edit Events: Click on event titles to add notes like room numbers or meeting spots
  • Delete Events: Remove events no one is interested in with the red "Delete" button
  • Split the Party: Use the attendee badges to coordinate who goes where during overlaps
